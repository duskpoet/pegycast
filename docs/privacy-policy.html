Pegycast Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

Pegycast is a podcast player application developed by Eduard Dykman. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

Pegycast does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Internet Access

The app connects to the internet solely to:

  • Fetch and parse podcast RSS feeds you subscribe to
  • Stream or download podcast audio episodes
  • Load podcast artwork images
  • Search for podcasts via third-party podcast directories

These requests go directly to the podcast publishers' servers and third-party podcast APIs. Pegycast does not route your data through its own servers.

Local Storage

The app stores your subscriptions, downloaded episodes, and playback progress locally on your device. This data is not transmitted anywhere and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Pegycast uses the Taddy API to provide podcast search functionality. Search queries are sent to Taddy's servers. Please refer to Taddy's own privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

Children's Privacy

Pegycast is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out by opening an issue on the Pegycast GitHub repository.
